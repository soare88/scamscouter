About ScamScouter

ScamScouter helps people check suspicious links, messages, emails and screenshots before they click, pay or share personal information.

Our mission

Our mission is to make scam checking simple for everyday users. ScamScouter highlights warning signs and gives practical guidance in a few seconds.

What we check

  • Suspicious links and unknown domains
  • Phishing and impersonation patterns
  • Fake delivery, marketplace, crypto and payment messages
  • Text extracted from screenshots through browser OCR

Important limitation

ScamScouter is an informational tool. No automated system can guarantee 100% accuracy. Always verify important payments and account requests through official channels.
