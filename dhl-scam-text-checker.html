How ScamScouter helps

ScamScouter reviews suspicious text, links, domains and message patterns to provide a clear risk score and practical safety advice before you pay.

Quick safety tips

  • Never pay customs or delivery fees via links sent from unknown phone numbers.
  • Always track your parcel directly on the official DHL website.
  • Be wary of generic messages that do not include your actual tracking number or name.
