Common risk signals

  • Unknown link or unusual domain pretending to be a major retailer.
  • Request for payment via wire transfer, crypto, or gift card.
  • Unrealistic countdown timers, low stock warnings, or discounts over 70%.
  • Missing physical address, phone number, or legitimate customer service email.

How ScamScouter helps

ScamScouter checks text, links, domains, emails and text extracted from screenshots. The result gives a risk score and clear explanations.

Quick safety tips

  • Do not enter card details on websites that do not use secure (HTTPS) connections.
  • Search for independent reviews of the store on platforms like Trustpilot before buying.
  • If the deal looks too good to be true, it almost always is. Treat it with caution.
