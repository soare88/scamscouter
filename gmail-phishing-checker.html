Common risk signals

  • Unknown link or unusual domain posing as Google security.
  • Request for account password, recovery code, or personal data.
  • Urgent warning claiming your account will be disabled in 24 hours.
  • Fake Google Drive or Docs link requiring you to sign in externally.

How ScamScouter helps

ScamScouter checks text, links, domains, emails and text extracted from screenshots. The result gives a risk score and clear explanations.

Quick safety tips

  • Do not enter your Google password or 2FA codes through links received in emails.
  • Always open your Google Account dashboard manually to check for genuine security alerts.
  • If the message creates urgency or threats, treat it with caution.
