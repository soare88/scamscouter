How ScamScouter Works

ScamScouter helps people check suspicious links, messages, emails and screenshots before they click, pay or share personal information.

What ScamScouter checks

  • Links, domains and suspicious URL patterns
  • Urgent language, payment requests and impersonation patterns
  • Text extracted from screenshots in the browser
  • Community reports approved through moderation

Security sources

The scanner uses ScamScouter rules, user reports and suspicious pattern detection. Optional external sources can be added later.

Important limitations

No automated tool can guarantee 100% accuracy. ScamScouter is designed to highlight warning signs and help you make safer decisions.
