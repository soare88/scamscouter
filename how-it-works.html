How ScamScouter Works

ScamScouter helps people check suspicious links, messages, emails and screenshots before they click, pay or share personal information.

What ScamScouter checks

🔗

Links and domains

We look for unknown domains, risky extensions, short links, brand impersonation and suspicious URL patterns.

📝

Message content

We check for urgent language, payment requests, verification codes, card requests, crypto promises and delivery scams.

🖼️

Screenshot OCR

When you upload a screenshot, the browser extracts text locally and sends the extracted text to the scanner.

Security sources

The scanner uses ScamScouter rules, user reports, suspicious pattern detection and optional Google Safe Browsing checks when configured.

Important limitations

No automated tool can guarantee 100% accuracy. ScamScouter is designed to highlight warning signs and help you make safer decisions, but you should always verify urgent payment or login requests through official channels.

Privacy-first image checks

For basic image checks, OCR runs in your browser. This keeps the process fast and reduces server costs. The text extracted from the screenshot can be edited before scanning.
