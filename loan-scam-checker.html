Common loan scam signs

  • Approval guaranteed before any real checks.
  • Upfront fee required to release the loan.
  • Payment requested by gift card, crypto, wire transfer or app.
  • Lender has no verifiable license or physical address.
  • Pressure to act immediately.

How to verify a lender

Search for the lender independently, verify registration where required, read independent reviews and avoid offers sent through unsolicited messages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is an upfront loan fee suspicious?

Yes. Advance fee loan scams often ask for money before releasing funds.

Can a lender guarantee approval?

Guaranteed approval with no checks is a warning sign.

What if I already paid a loan fee?

Contact your bank or payment provider quickly and save all messages and receipts.
