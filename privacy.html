Privacy Policy

ScamScouter is designed to help users check suspicious content while keeping privacy in mind.

Information you provide

When you scan a message, link or screenshot text, the submitted content may be processed to generate a risk result. If you report a scam, the report may be stored for moderation and safety improvement.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand traffic, page usage and product performance.

Safety

Do not submit passwords, card numbers, private keys, seed phrases or verification codes.
