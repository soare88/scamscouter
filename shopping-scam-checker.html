Shopping scam warning signs

  • prices far below normal market value;
  • new or unknown store with no clear company details;
  • fake reviews or repeated five-star comments;
  • checkout page on a different domain;
  • payment by wire transfer, crypto or gift card;
  • pressure such as countdown timers or limited stock claims.

Before you pay

Search for independent reviews, verify company details and scan the URL. If the deal looks too good to be true, stop and check.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if an online store is fake?

Check the domain, company details, reviews, return policy and payment method.

Are huge discounts always scams?

Not always, but unrealistic discounts on unknown websites are a major warning sign.

What if I paid a fake store?

Contact your bank quickly and save the order confirmation, URL and screenshots.
